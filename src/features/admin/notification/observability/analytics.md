# Outbound Analytics & OTP Tracker

Outbound logs are parsed daily to build aggregated volumes reports across channels and events categories.

## Metrics tracked
- **Delivery Success Rate**: Delivered notifications relative to total dispatched requests.
- **Hourly/Daily Volume Peaks**: Graphs delivery traffic spikes.
- **OTP Request & Abuse ratios**: Monitors verification rates, expirations, rapid requests blocks, and brute force suspects.
- **Transactional CTR & Open rates**: Maps transactional emails bounce ratios and links CTR tracking.
